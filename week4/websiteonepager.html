One Pager

Short pitch

The website is dedicated to the preservation of the history of video-games of the 1990s.

Long pitch

This website will deal with the history of the 1990s. It will showcase what console that the game was originated on and the exact date that it was released. It will also show newer games that have been released that are close in terms of game play and design to each specific video-games. It also provide information the developers and publishers of the video-games.

Personas

  • Bob the Enthusiast

    • This individual loves video-games and wants to know everything about them.

  • Jim the Newbie

    • This individual has taken an interest in video-games and is searching for information about them
